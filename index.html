The Seattle Seahawks are a pro football team from Seattle, Washington, playing in the NFLs NFC West division. They got their start back in 1976 and play their home games at Lumen Field, which is famous for its insanely loud and passionate fans—known as the “12th Man.” The team rocks colors of college navy, action green, and wolf gray, and their mascots are Blitz, plus a live hawk named Taima who leads the team out before games.
